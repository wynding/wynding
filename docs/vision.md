# Wynding — Vision

**Wynding is a deep, competitive maze game built to keep getting _better_ — open
source and community-tuned, so its balance, its meta, and its depth grow with the
players who master it.**

You build the maze: your towers _are_ the walls, and the creeps take the shortest
remaining path to the exit. The joy is optimization — replaying a board to find
the _better_ solve, climbing a leaderboard, and the "aha" when a non-obvious
strategy suddenly clicks and reframes the whole board. New metas emerge, the
community adapts, someone finds the next thing.

It's for the **optimizer** — the player delighted by a deep, legible system they
can master and re-solve, with the leaderboard as the long game — and for the
**tinkerers** who value a clean, forkable, moddable open-source game they can help
shape.

Open source is Wynding's **engine of longevity**: the community tunes the balance,
fixes imbalances, mods in new mechanics, and keeps the meta from ever calcifying —
so the game keeps growing for as long as people play it.

## Always

- **Deep, but approachable.** The skill ceiling is _strategic and knowledge-based_,
  never grind or twitch-execution — a newcomer who learns the meta can genuinely
  compete. No unbridgeable gap between newcomers and legends.
- **Fairly tuned.** Depth without cheap gotchas or feels-bad imbalances.
- **Open and community-evolvable.** It grows _with_ its players.

## Never

- Pay-to-win.
- RNG over skill.
- Closed source.
- Depth dumbed down to chase a broad casual crowd.
